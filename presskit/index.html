Description

We are an small indie game development studio without financial support based in Valladolid, Spain. Our studio is formed by people who loves videogames and have been enjoying them since they were born. Our main objetive is not only to make games, but to enjoy any step of its development process. If you share our dream and you want to join us, feel free to contact us.

History

The story starts when Arturo Casado and Sergio Escanciano were in highschool , learning how to program and design video games by themselves.

Through the years and after many failed attemps and unfinished projects, they decide to create an independent studio named Dark Respawn to develop their own ideas.

Years passed by. The lack of money, time and the inhability to find any people who could work with them in other disciplines, made the studio enter in an stand-by state.

In 2017, Arturo Casado releases Avoid The Walls (a game created by his own), under the Dark Respawn mark, trying to revive its name in order to restart some old projects and bring them new life and new ideas.

And now... Now we are working in new games and experiences which will come.
