Description

Avoid The Walls is an addictive ability game where you not only will face lots of challenges to get to the end of the level without touching anything, but also you should do it in the less time possible and achieving all the stars you can.

Features

  • 10 super-addictive levels with an stepped difficulty that will make you improve your skills.
  • Online records table with the acquired stars in each level.
  • 47 achievements.
  • Customizable player with up to 30 different models.
  • Replay's record system with online sharing option.
  • Funny visual design with cute enemies.
  • Different ways to complete the levels in or der to achieve better times.
  • Hours of fun guranteed if you try to complete the game at 100%.

About Dark Respawn Studio

Dark Respawn is an small indie game development studio without financial support based in Valladolid, Spain. Our studio is formed by people who loves videogames and have been enjoying them since they were born. Our main objetive is not only to make games, but to enjoy any step of its development process. If you share our dream and you want to join us, feel free to contact us.
